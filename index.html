TRAVEL TO THE NEXT LEVEL WITH WEB3 APPLICATIONS

Experience your next trip to the fullest with Monetrip’s Web3 applications. Monetrip offers you advanced solutions that get all the traveling hassles out of your head. Let’s see what we bring on the table.

FEATURES

DECENTRALIZED BOOKING

Booking accomodation, flight tickets, tour guide and more with whatever payment you choose, by Fiat currency or Crypto. We decentralize the data to make your booking experience effortless.

TRAVEL ASSISTANT

Planning your trip can't get any easier with our extraordinary collections of VR videos and powerful AI technology. All you need is to browse, our ​​algorithms would suggest the perfect trip for you, including destinations, accommodations, activities, and even tour guides.

VR EXPERIENCE

With our advanced VR videos, you can embark on an immersive journey through the most magnificent destinations of the world from the comfort of your home. Or check out your places of interest and its facilities before booking.

TRAVEL & EARN

Making money while enjoying traveling by QR tracking, creating captivating VR content, or simply by booking with us. The more you travel with us, the more you save and earn in our MONE token.
